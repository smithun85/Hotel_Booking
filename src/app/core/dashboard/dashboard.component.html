About Us

India as a country is struggling with the problem of employment in spite of having the largest young population in the world. As per the India Skills Report 2020, only 49 % Engineers, 54 % MBAs, 25% MCAs 47% B.com graduates are employable. The major reason behind this issue is the rising gap between Industry and academics. There is no integrated platform in India which is addressing this problem with the holistic approach. Skillspire is on a mission to make indian youth by providing them live training sessions on various skill sets.

Skill development without employment is of no use. We are committed to offer a relevant job, internship, WFH projects etc to our students. Along with a training program on skill development with an efficient assessment method, students get scoring. The more score a student has, the more active and career oriented he is considered to be. The efficient scoring system also helps recruiters to filter the student and select as per their preference.
